Last updated: June 7, 2026

1. Who We Are

Mochi Alarm is developed by Hubbe Inc. (contact: hubbeinc@gmail.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use Mochi Alarm ("the App") on Android. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Data We Store on Your Device

All your personal settings — alarms, ringtone preferences, repeat schedule, dark mode, pet name, streak count, and virtual items (Coins, Biscuits) — are stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that collect or store this data. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, or precise location.

3. Device Sensors

The App uses your device's accelerometer and gyroscope during the "Walk & Pour" mission to detect steps and tilt. Sensor data is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never recorded, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Mochi Alarm displays ads provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). To serve ads, Google may collect and use your device's Advertising ID (GAID), IP address, and general device information. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy (policies.google.com/privacy). You can reset or opt out of personalized ads at any time in your device Settings → Google → Ads → "Delete advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization." Upgrading to Mochi Alarm Premium removes all ads. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We may receive anonymous, aggregated crash reports and basic usage metrics through Google Play services (e.g., which screens crash). This data cannot identify you individually. You can opt out via Android Settings → Google → Manage your Google Account → Data & Privacy.

6. Notifications, Alarms & Device Permissions

To deliver alarms reliably, the App requests a set of device permissions that operate entirely on your phone and collect, store, or transmit no personal data: Notifications (to show the alarm and wake-up screen); Exact Alarms (so the alarm rings at the precise minute you set); Full-Screen Intent (to display the wake-up mission over your lock screen); Run at Startup / Boot Completed (to re-arm your saved alarms after a restart); and Vibration. With your optional, separate consent, the App may also ask to be excluded from battery optimization ("unrestricted" battery use) so the operating system does not delay or silence your alarm while the phone is idle. This battery exemption is entirely optional, is granted through your device's own system dialog, and can be revoked at any time in Android Settings. None of these permissions are used for advertising, tracking, or data collection.

7. Children's Privacy

Mochi Alarm is rated for general audiences (Everyone) and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. AdMob is configured to serve non-personalized ads only where required by law. If you believe a child under 13 is using the App and has submitted data, contact us at hubbeinc@gmail.com and we will take prompt corrective action.

8. Data Safety Summary (Google Play)

Data shared with third parties: Advertising ID may be shared with Google AdMob for ad serving. Data collected by us: None — all app data stays on your device. Security practices: Data is encrypted in transit by Google's ad network. You can request data deletion by uninstalling the App or deleting your Advertising ID in device settings.

9. Your Rights & Controls

You can delete all App data by uninstalling the App. You can opt out of personalized ads via Android device settings. EU/EEA users have rights under GDPR including access, erasure, and data portability — contact us at hubbeinc@gmail.com to exercise these rights. California residents have rights under CCPA; we do not sell personal information.

10. Security

We use industry-standard practices to keep the App secure. All network connections (such as ad requests) use HTTPS encryption. No personal data is stored on our servers. Use a screen lock on your device for additional protection.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
